# HR-ANALYTICS-DASHBOARD
Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence solution developed to analyze workforce demographics, employee attrition, and organizational performance. The dashboard provides HR managers and decision-makers with actionable insights into employee turnover trends, workforce composition, and retention challenges.

By visualizing key HR metrics and attrition patterns, the dashboard helps organizations identify areas requiring attention and develop data-driven strategies to improve employee retention and workforce planning.

Objectives
Monitor overall workforce statistics.
Track employee attrition and retention rates.
Analyze attrition across departments, job roles, education levels, and age groups.
Understand workforce demographics and employee distribution.
Support strategic HR decision-making through interactive visualizations.
